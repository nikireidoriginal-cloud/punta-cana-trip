Quick Tips

Waterpark

Aqua Nick closes at 5pm. Plan it for mornings.

Restaurants

Book à la carte reservations the moment you arrive — they fill up fast.

Beach

Strong currents at Uvero Alto — pools are the move. Swimmable beach is ~2km south.

Getting There

40-min drive from PUJ airport. I’ll book all private transport.

Health

Bring hand sanitizer and wipes. Wipe down high chairs.

Let’s Plan It Out

Once we pick a room setup, let’s pre-schedule what we want to do each day — waterpark mornings, pool time, a couple of nice dinners, spa, and lots of relaxing time built in.
